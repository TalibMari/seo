What is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization, which is the process of increasing the visibility of a website in search engine results pages. Good SEO helps your site rank higher and attract more organic visitors.

Image Optimization for SEO

Always use descriptive alt attributes, compress image sizes, and use modern formats like WebP for faster loading.

Video Content for SEO

Embedding videos improves engagement. Use keyword-rich titles and descriptions in the video metadata.

On-Page SEO Tips

  • Use clear and relevant headings (H1, H2, etc.)
  • Include your main keyword in the title, URL, and first paragraph
  • Create SEO-friendly meta descriptions
  • Link to authoritative external sites

Technical SEO Basics

Ensure your website is mobile-friendly, fast to load, and has clean URLs. Use structured data, an XML sitemap, and a robots.txt file to guide search engines.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does SEO require coding?

No, but basic HTML knowledge helps.

How long does SEO take to work?

It can take a few weeks to several months, depending on competition and effort.
